Team and Independent projects

Team collaborating is the most critcal part of team projects. One thing I have learned while working in a team is making sure all things that get brought up are documented so all the ideas are not lost. Another thing that I have learned while working in groups is no matter how small an idea may be, it can end up playing a major role in a project.

Graphic Design

Project
When it comes to design I like building from the ground up. This eagle head was created for a company, a group of develpers wanted a mascot that was agressive that signified there characters.What I really enjoyed about this project was the drawing that was required to render this mascot. I have always been a big fan of traditional art work. One of the biggest hurdles I had to work through while creating this image was the shadow for the eye. I had a hard time making an eye shadow that was visually pleasing without altering the eagles facial expressions. With that being said I believe it was a huge success because I still was able to capture the aggressiveness of the eagle while adding a small shadow above the eye.
